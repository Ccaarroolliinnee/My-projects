CAROLINE MARIA

Dancer / Teacher / Choreographer

ABOUT ME

Caroline found her passion for dance early at the age of 6. During her extensive formation, she was able to learn from renown teachers in Los Angeles, Sydney, Cali and Vienna. She completed a three month independent dance program at Peridance Carpezio Center New York City and trained Salsa at the famous Yamuleé dance studio. In LA she successfully auditioned for KreativMndz Academy and was fortunate to receive individual training with their famous teachers. Her versatility define her as a dancer, she is educated in Street Styles, Commercial, Voguing, Dancehall, House, Brazilian Zouk, Samba and Salsa.

Her teaching experience spans over 10 years just like her choreographing projects. She furthermore directed and produced many concept videos.
